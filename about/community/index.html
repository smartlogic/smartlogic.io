We believe in good corporate citizenship.

We give back to the community in a number of ways, including sponsoring meetups and conferences, charitable giving, and volunteering.

  • Apprenticeships

    In 2018 we ran a very successful apprenticeship program, bringing three promising Junior-level developers up to the skill level needed for the kind of work we do.

  • MicroGrants and Donations

    Our latest community program is the COVID SLAM; other programs from the recent past include $10k to Black-led organizations in 2020 and the Baltimore Women in Tech Micro Grant program which ran in 2017 and 2018.

  • Volunteering

    SmartLogic team members contribute to a number of initiatives as volunteers, including open source development, organizing civic hack events like Hack Baltimore, and in-person volunteering at events like the Hour of Code.

  • Conferences

    We attend, present at, and sponsor a number of national and regional tech events including ElixirConf, RESTFest, and ChainReact.

  • Open Source

    We’re committed to open source; we only use open source technologies in our applications.

  • Local Events

    We’ve been sponsoring meetups in Baltimore since 2010; we sponsored 73 events in 2018 alone.

Working at SmartLogic

What do we have to offer? We're glad you asked. Here are a few things that set us apart:

  • Flexibility

    Our office hours are 10am - 3:30pm; work the rest of your 40 hours whenever is most convenient for you. We've also had employees work abroad for extended periods of time.

  • Home Office Support

    We're all remote these days; we allocate budget to make sure your home work office is comfortable and well-stocked with whatever you need to be most productive.

  • Strong Technical Talent

    Work with other technical people. Everyone on the team codes, from the president down. Designers/developers do the interviewing and have the ultimate say on who joins the team.

  • Benefits

    We offer competitive pay, vacation time, medical + dental + vision insurance. We offer a 3% SIMPLE IRA match. We send folks to conferences throughout the year. Everyone gets new equipment (15” MacBook Pro’s).

  • Low Stress; Interesting Work

    You get to work on the interesting technical challenges. We continuously optimize our PM and software development processes for low stress and mitigation of the mundane.

  • Good Corporate Citizens

    We have an annual charity giving program and have sponsored dozens of conferences and meetups. Several of our employees are also active non-profit board members and volunteers.

We've built over 150 applications since 2005.

Whether you're starting a company or you're a global brand launching an iPhone app, our team can help you out.

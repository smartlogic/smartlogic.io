Elixir Wizards Podcast

Season 13: The Creators' Lab

In Season 13, the Elixir Wizards venture into the “Creators’ Lab” to speak with innovators expanding the boundaries of what’s possible with the Elixir Programming Language. Hosts Sundi, Owen, Dan, and Charles interview the creators of various projects that inspire and challenge the status quo.

This season dives into how these groundbreaking projects are transforming industries—from IoT and machine learning to large-scale web applications—while offering listeners a glimpse into the future of Elixir's role in shaping technology.

Tune in to explore the magic happening at the intersection of creativity, engineering, and Elixir.

Season 13 began airing in October 2024.

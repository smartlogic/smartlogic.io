Season 15: The State of the Stack

In Season 15, the Elixir Wizards discuss current events, shifts in the software development landscape, and how the community is adapting in response. Hosts Sundi, Charles, and Emma chat with Elixirists who are at the forefront of addressing these changes and coming up with viable solutions for the future.

Season Fifteen airs from April 2026 to July 2026.

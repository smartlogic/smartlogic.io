Working with Us

There are a number of things you can expect when you hire us. Everything we do is focused on one thing: providing the best possible value for your investment.

  • Constant Communication

    When you work with Smartlogic, you have direct access to the developers working on your project. There’s no middle man: you always talk with the web and mobile developers who best understand your project.

  • High-Levels of Organization

    Whether developing software, mobile applications or web applications, we our team is highly-organized, ensuring you have a transparent view of exactly where your project stands at all times.

  • Good Planning

    Before we begin an iteration of work we ensure we have thoroughly defined the work we'll complete, thereby delivering better, more effective software for our clients.

  • The Best Code

    Our team of experienced designers and developers follows best practices–including using source code control, behavior- and test-driven development, pair programming, continuous integration, and more.

Trusted Technology for Fast, Global Impact

World Central Kitchen partnered with SmartLogic to build a national food distribution platform designed to scale quickly and operate reliably during Covid. A unified platform allowed them to expand from 3 cities to over 10, more than doubling the number of participating restaurants in their text-to-order food program.

Within weeks, the system connected local restaurants and families in need across the U.S., delivering more than one million meals in its first year.

WCK Direct partnered with independent, community-based restaurants like Barcha (NYC), Three Amigos (Charlotte, NC), and dozens more. Each city's roster featured locally owned eateries representing diverse cuisines which often included minority-owned or community-based restaurants.

Real-time visibility into meals served per city, per dollar, and per partner allowed WCK to expand where help was needed most.

Challenge.gov: Innovation at Scale

SmartLogic built a software platform for Challenge.gov that enables government agencies to run innovation challenges and engage participants nationwide. The scalable, cloud-based system allows crowdsourced solutions from the public through open competitions.

To date, Challenge.gov has facilitated over 800 challenges and engaged 1 million participants.

Our open-source platform transformed Challenge.gov into a true hub for government innovation, enabling agencies to launch and manage challenges independently while engaging more citizens than ever before.

This is transformative because it moves beyond traditional internal structures and allows anyone to contribute ideas to the federal government. Challenge.gov proves that collaboration drives stronger solutions.

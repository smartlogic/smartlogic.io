FolioCollaborative

FolioCollaborative provides a comprehensive tool for managing the faculty evaluation and growth process in independent schools. Our teams worked in tandem to determine which features would have the highest benefit for the product's initial release. We also helped to identify goals for the entire project: security, easy web-based reporting, and importing schools' existing data.

GiveCorps

GiveCorps is a social website headquartered in Baltimore that inspires givers of all ages and incomes to support local nonprofit organizations rewards donors with deals and discounts from local restaurants and retailers. We developed the entire application - including features to facilitate e-commerce transactions and features allowing administrators to manage available projects that can be funded.

View 3 screenshots Go to http://givecorps.com

SpotCrime

SpotCrime is the most comprehensive online source of crime information. SpotCrime has a database of over 15 million crimes and an email subscriber base of over 300,000 users. We designed and built mechanisms to allow SpotCrime to withstand large influxes of users and have failover capabilities should a server go down.

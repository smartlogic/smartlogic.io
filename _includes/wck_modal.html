World Central Kitchen

World Central Kitchen (WCK) provides meals to those who need it, in times of crisis and beyond.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, WCK developed a new offering, WCK Direct, that connects households experiencing food insecurity to restaurants.

SmartLogic built a robust Elixir Phoenix and Flutter app to power the WCK Direct program, supporting the program's growth to additional cities, and improving stability, scale, and usability over their off-the-shelf MVP.

Services
  • iPad Flutter restaurant app
  • Twilio integration allows recipients to order via phone or text in English and Spanish
  • Program admins now have tooling and dashboards to manage the program
  • Active in 7 cities and with plans to expand

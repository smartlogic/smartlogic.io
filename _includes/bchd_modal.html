Baltimore City Health Department

The Baltimore City Health Department (BCHD) is the oldest continuously-operating health department in the United States.

SmartLogic worked on BCHD's CHARMcare platform, an open-source directory that guides users to free or low-cost resources in Baltimore. SmartLogic conducted user research and implemented a number of search and usability improvements to CHARMcare, including integrating Elasticsearch, rank-weighting results, geographic filtering, improved tagging, and file uploads.

Services
  • Integrated Elasticsearch to support more complex search ranking while improving performance and speed
  • Conducted user research to help BCHD define the most impactful improvements
  • Built with Ruby on Rails, deployed to Heroku for ease of maintenance

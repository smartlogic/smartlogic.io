World Central Kitchen

World Central Kitchen (WCK) provides meals to those who need it, in times of crisis and beyond.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, WCK developed a new offering, WCK Direct, that connects households experiencing food insecurity to restaurants.

SmartLogic built a robust Elixir Phoenix and Flutter app to power the WCK Direct program, supporting the program's growth to additional cities, and improving stability, scale, and usability over their off-the-shelf MVP.

Services
  • iPad Flutter restaurant app
  • Twilio integration allows recipients to order via phone or text in English and Spanish
  • Program admins now have tooling and dashboards to manage the program
  • Active in 7 cities and with plans to expand

Challenge.Gov

Challenge.Gov is a GSA-run program that allows any federal agency to engage with the public via challenges and prize competitions.

The new open-source platform built by SmartLogic automates previously manual processes, and integrates with GSA TTS systems like Login.gov, Cloud.gov, and Search.gov.

The Challenge team is now for the first time able to get visibility into how stakeholders are engaging with the program. Additionally, the new self-serve tools have helped the program's adoption to grow and reach new users.

Services
  • Elixir/Phoenix, ReactJS, Jekyll
  • Over 7,000 users as of Jan 2022
  • Described by our contact at GSA as "the Army Jeep of applications — it's very simple. It does exactly what you want it to — it's intuitive, clean, simple."

Baltimore City Health Department

The Baltimore City Health Department (BCHD) is the oldest continuously-operating health department in the United States.

SmartLogic worked on BCHD's CHARMcare platform, an open-source directory that guides users to free or low-cost resources in Baltimore. SmartLogic conducted user research and implemented a number of search and usability improvements to CHARMcare, including integrating Elasticsearch, rank-weighting results, geographic filtering, improved tagging, and file uploads.

Services
  • Integrated Elasticsearch to support more complex search ranking while improving performance and speed
  • Conducted user research to help BCHD define the most impactful improvements
  • Built with Ruby on Rails, deployed to Heroku for ease of maintenance
